Hydrabad


Government initiated a project to develop 1,100,000 sq ft (100,000 m2) of IT space and 450,000 sq ft (42,000 m2) of residential space with private investors. MEDHA was the first operational phase of the project. This area became the major IT hub of the city equipped with IT parks and special economic zones.
